More Than Another Recipe Website

Cookr is a tool to help you reach your goal. If you want to lose weight, gain weight, maintainweight, gain muscle, etc, we are here for you. You and your goals are our top priority

How it Works

1. Choose Your Goals

When you register your account, you'll fill out a form with some basic information including your goals. This ensures that we can help you find recipes that fit your lifestyle and bring you closer to your best self.

2. Choose Your Recipe

After you sign up and log in, you will be able to see recipe recomendations. Simply swipe left (if you don't like it) or right (if you do) on them. The ones you like will be saved.

3. Let Us Choose For You

Over time we learn what you like and what you don't like. The more you use Cookr, wthe better we will be able to recomend you food you love.

What makes COOKR unique

COOKR is designed to work with you to help you reach your goal. Our product works by finding recipes for you to swipe on depending on what your fitness goals are and what your food preferences are. Overtime we will learn what type of food you like and don't like and recommend foods that you are more likely to like first.
